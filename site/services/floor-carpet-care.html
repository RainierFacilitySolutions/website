Floor & Carpet Care

Deep extraction, refinishing, and stain removal for commercial carpet, tile, hardwood, vinyl, and concrete. Cleaner floors. Healthier facilities. Longer-lasting investments.

Our method

Hot-water extraction, done right.

Most floor care vendors run a single quick pass and call it done. We pre-treat, agitate, extract at high temperature, and finish with a low-residue rinse — so carpets dry faster and stay cleaner longer.

For tough stains (coffee, ink, food, biohazard) we apply targeted spotters before the main extraction, not after, so we don't drive the stain deeper into the fibers.

Why it matters

Cleaner floors mean a healthier facility.

Indoor air quality

Dust, allergens, and microbes accumulate deep in carpet fibers. Hot-water extraction pulls them out — vacuuming alone won't.

Asset protection

Embedded grit acts like sandpaper on carpet fibers and hard floors. Routine professional care extends the life of every floor type — and your capital budget.

First impressions

Tenants, clients, and inspectors notice your floors before they notice anything else. A polished lobby is part of your brand.
