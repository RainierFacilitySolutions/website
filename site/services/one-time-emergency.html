One-Time & Emergency Cleaning

Sometimes you don't need a recurring contract — you need someone reliable, fast, and ready today. That's us.

When to call

Single-visit cleans, handled like our regular accounts.

You don't have to be a recurring client to get our best work. We dispatch the same trained crews and the same standards to one-time engagements — whether you're prepping for a board meeting, recovering from a flood, or trying to wow a new tenant on move-in day.

For true emergencies (water damage, biohazard, sewage backup), call 253-327-3242 directly — we route emergency calls to dispatch so we can respond fast.

Common one-time jobs

  • Deep clean before a property tour or inspection
  • Pre-event & post-event cleanup
  • Tenant turnover / unit make-ready
  • Seasonal & year-end deep clean
  • Construction punch-list cleaning
  • Trial clean before signing a recurring contract
Emergency response
