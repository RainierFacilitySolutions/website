Post-Construction Cleaning

The job's not done until the dust is gone. We get new commercial builds and renovations photo-, inspection-, and tenant-ready.

For builders, GCs & property owners

The final 5% — handled.

Drywall dust gets everywhere. It settles into vents, light fixtures, inside cabinets, and onto every horizontal surface — including the ones nobody thought to look at. We clear it all in a methodical, top-to-bottom pass that respects your finishes and your timeline.

We coordinate directly with your superintendent or project manager, work around punch-list trades when needed, and can stage the clean in phases (rough → final → touch-up) for larger jobs.

Get a Free Quote →

Three-phase cleaning

Rough clean — debris removal, sweeping, large dust knockdown after framing/drywall.

Final clean — top-to-bottom detail clean once finishes are installed.

Touch-up clean — final pass before walkthrough, photography, or tenant move-in. Removes any dust raised during punch-list work.

What we handle

The full post-construction scope.

Surfaces & finishes

  • Drywall dust on all surfaces, vents, returns & ledges
  • Paint splatter on glass, tile, hardwood & trim
  • Adhesive residue, stickers & protective film
  • Light fixtures, fans, recessed cans
  • Inside & outside all cabinets & millwork
  • Doors, frames, baseboards, trim

Glass, floors & fixtures

  • Windows, sliders, mirrors — interior & exterior
  • Window tracks, frames, sills detailed
  • Hard floors swept, mopped, polished
  • Carpet vacuumed (extraction available)
  • Restrooms — full sanitation, fixtures polished
  • Break rooms & kitchens — appliance interiors, sinks, hardware
