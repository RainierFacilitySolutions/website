Build a career, not just a paycheck.

We're hiring trained, reliable, and genuinely good people across the Greater Seattle Area. Stable hours, paid training, real growth — and a team that actually has your back.

Why work here

A cleaning job that respects your time.

Most cleaning companies treat employees as interchangeable. We don't. Our crews are W-2 employees, not subcontractors. We invest in training, we pay on time, and we build schedules around real lives — not the other way around.

Whether you're starting your first job or you've been in this industry for 20 years, there's a clear path here: cleaner → lead → supervisor → account manager. We promote from within, and most of our supervisors started on the floor.

What we offer

  • W-2 employment — taxes withheld, real records
  • Paid training — on the clock from day one
  • Consistent weekly schedules
  • Mileage reimbursement between sites
  • Eco-friendly products provided
  • Promotion from within — leads, supervisors, account managers
  • Bilingual-friendly workplace (English & Spanish)
  • Family-owned, locally operated — owners answer the phone

How we work

What you can expect from us.

Honest schedules

We don't change your shifts at the last minute. If something does shift, you hear from us — not a no-call surprise.

Paid training

You're on the clock during training. We'd rather invest the hours up front than send you out unprepared.

Safe equipment

Modern, well-maintained gear. Eco-friendly products. PPE provided. Heavy-lift jobs aren't done solo.

Real teammates

Small, stable crews. You'll know your coworkers, your supervisor, and the owner — by first name.

A path forward

Most of our supervisors started as cleaners. If you want to grow, we'll meet you halfway.

Open communication

Direct line to your manager. We listen when something's wrong — and we fix it.
