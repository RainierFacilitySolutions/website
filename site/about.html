About Rainier Facility Solutions

A locally owned commercial cleaning company built on consistency, trust, and the kind of attention to detail you can actually feel when you walk through the door.

Our story

Built in Sumner. Trusted across the Greater Seattle Area.

Rainier Facility Solutions started with a simple frustration: facility cleaning vendors that came in strong, did a great job once, then quietly let standards slip. Our founders set out to build the opposite — a cleaning company where the same trained crew shows up every visit, the standard never drops, and the price stays predictable.

Fifteen-plus years and thousands of cleans later, we're the team facility managers, property owners, and operations leads keep on speed-dial. We serve offices, medical practices, retail, industrial, and multi-tenant properties from Tacoma through Everett.

We're not a franchise. The owners answer the phone. Our crews are W-2 employees — not subcontractors — and every one of them is bonded, insured, and background-checked before their first visit.

Our Values

What we mean when we say "trusted."

Trust before everything

You're letting us into your building, often after hours. We treat that like the privilege it is — every team member is screened, every key is logged, every code is private.

Consistency over heroics

Anyone can clean a building once. The hard part is doing it the same way, the same well, on visit number 47. Our written scopes and supervisor walkthroughs keep standards tight.

Eco by default

We default to products that are safe for tenants, employees, and visitors. Strong enough for grease and grout, gentle enough for the people in your building.

Respect your time

Clear schedules. Honest updates if traffic or weather pushes us late. Off-hours and weekend service is the norm, not a premium add-on.

Hire local. Pay local.

Our team lives in the same neighborhoods we clean. Hiring us puts wages back into Pierce, King, and Snohomish counties.

Stay accountable

If we miss something, tell us within 24 hours. We come back, no charge. The guarantee isn't a marketing line — it's how we run the business.

Credentials & Trust

Licensed & insured

Registered Washington State business. General liability and workers' comp coverage on file — certificates of insurance available on request before your first visit.
